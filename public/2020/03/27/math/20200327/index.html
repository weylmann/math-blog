如图所示, 已知四边形ABCDABCDABCD 为正方形, △AEF\triangle AEF△AEF 为等边三角形, E,ME,ME,M 在线
段BCBCBC 上且BE=CMBE=CMBE=CM, FFF 在线段CDCDCD 上, ACACAC 与EFEFEF 交于点NNN.

(1) 证明: S△ABE+S△ADF=S△CEFS_{\triangle ABE}+S_{\triangle ADF}=S_{\triangle CEF}S​△ABE​​+S​△ADF​​=S​△CEF​​;
(2) 证明: NM∥AENM\parallel AENM∥AE.

参考思路

显然有△ABE≅△ADF(HL)⇒CE=CF,AC⊥EF\triangle ABE\cong \triangle ADF(HL)\Rightarrow CE=CF, AC\bot EF△ABE≅△ADF(HL)⇒CE=CF,AC⊥EF.
设EN=FN=a⇒NC=a,AE=AF=2aEN=FN=a\Rightarrow NC=a, AE=AF=2aEN=FN=a⇒NC=a,AE=AF=2a, 由勾股定理可得CE=CF=2a,NA=3a,AB=BC=6+22aCE=CF=\sqrt{2}a, NA=\sqrt{3}a, AB=BC=\frac{\sqrt{6}+\sqrt{2}}{2}aCE=CF=√​2​​​a,NA=√​3​​​a,AB=BC=​2​​√​6​​​+√​2​​​​​a. 所以可得BE=6−22aBE=\frac{\sqrt{6}-\sqrt{2}}{2}aBE=​2​​√​6​​​−√​2​​​​​a.

所以: S△ABE=12⋅6+22a⋅6−22a=a22=S△CENS_{\triangle ABE}=\frac{1}{2}\cdot \frac{\sqrt{6}+\sqrt{2}}{2}a\cdot \frac{\sqrt{6}-\sqrt{2}}{2}a =\frac{a^2}{2}=S_{\triangle CEN}S​△ABE​​=​2​​1​​⋅​2​​√​6​​​+√​2​​​​​a⋅​2​​√​6​​​−√​2​​​​​a=​2​​a​2​​​​=S​△CEN​​. 所以(1)得证.
